Overview

Design and Display

The Dell Inspiron 14 5430 sports a sleek aluminum chassis with a compact and lightweight build, ideal for professionals and students on the move. It features a 14-inch Full HD+ (1920 x 1200) anti-glare display with a 16:10 aspect ratio, offering a more vertical screen space and comfortable viewing angles.

Performance and Productivity

Equipped with a 13th Gen Intel Core i7-1355U processor, this laptop handles demanding multitasking, productivity software, and light creative work with ease. With 16GB LPDDR5 RAM and a massive 1TB SSD, you get ultra-fast boot times, quick file access, and efficient overall performance.

Connectivity & Features

  • Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2
  • Full HD webcam with noise reduction and dual microphones
  • Backlit keyboard and fingerprint reader for secure login
  • Ports: USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, microSD card reader
  • Windows 11 Home pre-installed
